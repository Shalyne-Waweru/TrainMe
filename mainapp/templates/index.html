Our Services
Obedience Training

Obedience training will involve training
your dog to obey basic commands that
are necessary for keeping proper control
of the dog.

Protection Training

Protection training will involve training
your dog to attack people.This is only
applicable to those licensed security
guards that are eligible to have their
dogs trained as protection dogs

Behaviour Modification

Behaviour Modification training will help
in Behaviour Modification training will help
in changing the undesirable behaviors
that your dog exhibits

Puppy Training

Puppy training will involve simple sessions
that involve the use of positive
reinforcement techniques

Service Training

Service training is a technique that involves
training your dog to perform specific
tasks on cue for the benefit of people
with disabilities.

Agility Training

Dog agility training will involve a sport
where your dog will be directed through
a pre-set obstacle course within a
certain time limit
